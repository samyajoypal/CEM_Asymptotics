# Focused external-reader check

Before submission, ask a statistician who has not worked on the paper to read
only the Introduction, Theorem 1, Theorem 6, and the across-coordinate
simulation-quantile table.

Ask: “Is the contribution and its scope clear without reading the rest of the
paper? What claim, assumption, or empirical result remains ambiguous?” Record
the reader's name, date, and unedited response here before submission. This is
an outstanding human review task and has not been represented as completed by
an automated check.
